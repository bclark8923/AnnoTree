AnnoTree is a new collaboration tool for helping businesses build better mobile applications.
We live in a mobile world where developers and testers often work across the globe rather than down the hall. Our team saw the need for a streamlined solution that brings together developers, designers, testers, and managers on the same platform.

How It Works

  • The AnnoTree Widget allows anyone to collaborate by drawing or writing directly on the application.
  • Once satisfied, the user can send these annotations (i.e. "Leaf"s) off to our Central Collaboration Platform.
  • Here, team members can organize, view, and collaborate on these Leaves across multiple devices.
