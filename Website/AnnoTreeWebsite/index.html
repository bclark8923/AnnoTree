AnnoTree is a new collaboration tool for helping businesses build better mobile applications.
We live in a mobile world where developers and testers often work across the globe rather than down the hall and saw the need for a streamlined solution that brings together developers, designers, testers, and owners on the same platform.

How It Works

  • The AnnoTree Widget allows anyone to collaborate by drawing or writing directly on the application.
  • Once satisfied, the user can send these annotations (i.e. "Leaf"s) off to our Central Collaboration Platform.
  • Here, team members can organize, view, and collaborate on these Leaves across multiple devices.

Why AnnoTree

We've built applications just like you. We've felt the pain, time loss, money loss, and collaboration issues around working across many devices, many timezones and many customers.
