Organizing the Unorganized

Our Team comes from over 30 combined years of programming experience across multiple platforms. Upon moving into developing in the mobile space we found many pains in collecting feedback directly from users with full context, both visually and programatically.

To solve this solution we went out to build a platform that would give us, and many others, a much faster, simpler, more organized way of collecting and reviewing design and bug flaws in a single, collaborative platform for any device and any situation.

The Product

  • The AnnoTree SDK allows anyone to draw or write directly on any mobile application to denote a design flaw or bug.
  • They can then send these "leafs" off to our central collaboration platform
  • Here the application creators can organize and collaborate on these "leafs" across multiple devices.
