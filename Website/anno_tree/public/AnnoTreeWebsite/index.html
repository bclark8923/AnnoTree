Collaboration Anywhere

Our lives today are mobile and our developers, testers, and more are often not in the same location. Many tools exist to help businesses create applications, manage their time and collaborate on on projects, but the problem of developers, designers, testers and owners all being able to work efficiently together from anywhere still exists.

We wanted the ability to collaborate on application creation on any device, at any time, without having to leave our application. This is why we created AnnoTree. A mobile first platform delivering a single location for entire teams to collaborate and streamline their application creation process.

The Product

  • The AnnoTree SDK allows anyone to draw or write directly on any mobile application to denote a design flaw or bug.
  • They can then send these "leafs" off to our central collaboration platform
  • Here the application creators can organize and collaborate on these "leafs" across multiple devices.
