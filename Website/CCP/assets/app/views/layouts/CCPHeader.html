How To Use AnnoTree

Collaboration On Application Development

AnnoTree is a tool for organizing and collaborating on application creation. At a high level we organize things into Forests (your organization), Trees (your projects), and Leaves (parts of your projects).

Forest

At the top level AnnoTree has forests. This represents your company or organization for organizing all of your projects, or as we call them trees, together.

Trees

Inside forests you have your trees. These represent applications or projects you are currently working on and hold all of the "leaves" that represent parts of your application.

Leaves

At the lowest level you have the various leaves on a tree. These leaves hold the screenshots and annotations on your applications to collaborate on as a team.
